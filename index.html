Favorite Restaurants On Saipan

When it comes to traveling, one of the most important aspects of the experience is grabbing a bite at the local restaurants. Saipan is no exception to this so here are my three personal favorite restaurants here on island.

  1. American Pizza & Grill
  2. Shirley's Coffee Shop
  3. Java Joes

American Pizza & Grill | Garapan

If you love eating burgers like me, American Pizza & Grill is a must try spot here on island. They serve a variety of tasty burgers as well as other dishes like pizza, fried chicken, pasta, and various beverages aswell.

What makes this place great?

  • A wide range of burgers to choose from
  • Fantastic pizza with options to make it your own
  • Delicious milkshakes

Shirley's Coffee Shop | Garapan

Famous for its fried rice and a wide range of dishes from crispy pata to beef short ribs, Shirley's Coffee Shop is a great place to dine out during either breakfast, lunch, or dinner.

What makes this place great?

  • Famous fried rice
  • Wide variety of dishes from local to Filipino favorites
  • NMC ProaPerk's members can save 10% off and have complimentary reffilable iced tea when they dine on Mondays

Java Joes | Dandan

Need a quick bite before class? Java Joe's got you covered. With its close location to NMC, its a great place to grab a cold frappe and a burger to fill you up before class time.

What makes this place great?

  • Close proximity to NMC
  • Variety of cold drinks and comfort foods available
  • Cozy dining area
